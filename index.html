About Me

Hello, I’m Ibrahim Robiu, the brain behind IR Analytics. I’m a data analyst who loves turning messy data into meaningful stories using tools like Excel, SQL, Power BI, Tableau, and Python.

I enjoy working with real-world datasets—whether it’s sales, healthcare, customer behavior, or finance. My projects focus on data cleaning, exploration, and visualization to help businesses and individuals make smarter decisions.
